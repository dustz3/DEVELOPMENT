# Depot & Distribution

## Partner Location: Australia
TailorMed offers comprehensive logistics solutions tailored to the unique needs of CROs, biotech companies, pharmaceutical manufacturers, and depot distributors. Our expertise spans both domestic and international markets, ensuring seamless operations from start to finish.

To better serve our valued local clients, we've partnered with a leading warehousing and fulfillment provider in Australia. This strategic alliance empowers us to offer a full suite of services, including:

- **GMP-compliant warehousing:**
Ensuring the highest standards of product handling and storage. 

- **emperature-controlled storage:**
Maintaining optimal conditions from controlled-room to ultra-low temperature for your valuable products.

- **Inventory management:**
Accurate tracking and monitoring of your stock.

Efficient processing and shipping of orders.

Customized services like kitting and assembly.
